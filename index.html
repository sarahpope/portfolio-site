Sarah Pope

Product and Content Designer

I’m a designer working remotely from Cincinnati, OH. I enjoy helping businesses solve tough problems with human-focused solutions.

I have spent most of my adult life captivated by the internet and how we interact with it. Currently, I’m on my third year at Hireology. I work with an amazing team designing software that helps people build better teams.

I take on freelance projects when I have the bandwidth and I especially enjoy working with clients attempting to do some good in the world. Sound like you? We should talk.

As a designer, I’m all about implementing what’s best for the end user—not my ego. Solving systemic problems while simultaneously creating something beautiful can feel pretty magical.

When I’m not working on web, you can find me exploring my new city, herding my two pups, or trying out new hobbies.
